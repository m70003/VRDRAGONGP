VR DRAGON GP was born with the vision of revolutionizing the world of motor racing by combining traditional motor racing with virtual reality (VR) technology. This pioneering project focuses on providing a unique entertainment experience that not only appeals to motor racing fans, but also to technology enthusiasts and those interested in new forms of entertainment.


The purpose of VR DRAGON GP is clear and ambitious: to create the best possible single-seater entertainment within the context of Formula Student, using technology and innovation as fundamental pillars. We want to set a new standard in the industry, where high quality engineering is combined with immersive experiences, providing a spectacle that transcends the limits of traditional racing.
